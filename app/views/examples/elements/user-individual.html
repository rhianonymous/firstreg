Type of vehicle you are registering


What is classed as a new vehicle

A vehicle that is a current model or a model that ceased production within the last 2 years. It has not been sold as retail before and only has delivery milage on the clock.


What is classed as an import

A new or used vehicle being brought into the UK for 6 months or over in a 12 month rolling period.


What is classed as an rebuild

A vehicle that has been rebuilt using a mix of noth new and used parts.


What is classed as a kit built vehicle

A kit built vehicle is one where all the parts of a vehicle are supplied new by the manufacturer. A Kit conversion is a vehicle where a kit of new parts is added to an existing vehicle or old parts are added to a new kit.
